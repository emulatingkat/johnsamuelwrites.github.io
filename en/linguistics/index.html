Linguistics

John Samuel

I am no linguist, but linguistics is one of my favourite subjects. Despite having no formal training in this field, I follow this subject and love its advancements. As a person who can speak, write and read more than three different languages with varying levels of fluency, I love the diversity of languages and what they offer.
